E-commerce

Computers becoming so widely used has led to the rise of e-commerce giants such as Amazon overtaking traditional retailers. Although this has had negative effects on local businesses, it has also made shopping extremely convenient for the average consumer, especially those who struggle to go shopping in person due to location, age, or disability.

Payment technology

The invention of new digital wallets such as Google Pay has made it easier to pay both online and in person. In person, you can pay with your phone instead of your card, and online, you no longer have to enter your details every time you purchase something.
